### Recommended Specifications:
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: 800x800px or larger (square works best for gallery)
- **Aspect ratio**: 1:1 (square) recommended
- **File size**: Under 300KB for fast loading
- **Content**: Show the window film on different car windows, installation process, close-ups, before/after, etc.

### Supported Formats:
- `.jpg` ✅
- `.jpeg` ✅
- `.png` ✅
- `.webp` ✅

### How to Add:
1. Save your product images to this folder: `public/images/products/`
2. Name them exactly as listed above (case-sensitive)
3. Refresh your browser - the images will appear automatically in the product gallery

### Image Ideas for Gallery:
- Close-up of window film on car glass
- Different angles of installation
- Before/after comparisons
- Film on different car models
- Installation process steps
- Product packaging
- Film sample/roll
- Detail shots of film texture

### Note:
- If an image doesn't exist, a placeholder will be shown automatically
- You can add 1-4 images per product (all 4 slots will show placeholders until images are added)
- Images will display in a 2x2 grid on mobile, 4-column grid on desktop
